DeFi Basics

What is APY?
APY (Annual Percentage Yield) represents the real rate of return on your investment, taking into account the effect of compounding interest.
Understanding Impermanent Loss
Impermanent loss occurs when providing liquidity to AMMs. It's the difference between holding tokens vs providing liquidity when prices change.
Compound Interest Power
Compound interest is earning interest on your interest. The more frequently you compound, the faster your investment grows.

Risk Warnings

  • Smart contract risks - Always audit protocols before investing
  • Impermanent loss can exceed farming rewards
  • High APYs often indicate higher risk
  • Gas fees can significantly impact small investments
  • Always DYOR (Do Your Own Research)

DeFi Glossary

TVL

Total Value Locked - The total value of assets deposited in a protocol

AMM

Automated Market Maker - Protocol that enables decentralized trading

LP Token

Liquidity Provider Token - Represents your share in a liquidity pool

Yield Farming

Earning rewards by providing liquidity or staking tokens

Slippage

Difference between expected and executed price in a trade

Flash Loan

Uncollateralized loan that must be repaid in the same transaction
